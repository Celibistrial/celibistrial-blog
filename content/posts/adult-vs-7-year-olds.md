## How many children can an adult (you) fight at once

### The assumptions

*   Adult is under 25 (he is at peak condition with no disabilities or crippling diseases)
*   The children are below 12 years
*   The adult and the children are not trained in any forms of combat
*   The children attack the adult all at once and have no regard for their lives
*   Pain tolerance can be ignored as both the adult and the children will likely have adrenaline pumping through their body

### How most people think the fight would play out

> "Yeah I could probably take on 20–30 kids at once, what are they going to do? I am much stronger than them and could probably knock each one out with a single punch"

### How the fight would probably play out

Since we are working with the assumption that all the kids will attack you at once and have no regard for their lives, it is safe to say that you would start struggling to fight more than 10 kids at once.

It would be fairly easy to dispatch under 5 kids as you would be able to keep track of the positions of all the kids, but as this number grows to lets say 15 or 20, it would become much harder to keep track of their positions, and you would probably find your self surrounded.

It is important to keep in mind that while you are much stronger than the kids, every time you punch\\kick them you also have a **chance** of breaking your hand\\leg. Their bones are weaker compared to yours but punching them with full force to the skull (or any other part of the body which is deeply protected) would probably hurt your hand and even has a fair chance of breaking it.

Another thing to keep in mind is that **children can easily bite flesh off of a human**. So if these kids happen to surround you, you have probably already lost the fight. A group of kids (10+) could easily get behind you and bite your calves or hands and while they may not be able to rip it off completely, they will still inflict significant damage which would also reduce your fighting capabilities, and you would also start losing blood.
